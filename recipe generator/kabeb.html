Kabab

Kabab is a popular grilled meat dish in Middle Eastern cuisine, made from ground meat (typically lamb, beef, or chicken), spices, and herbs, skewered and grilled over a charcoal flame.

prep time

45 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags: grilled lamb Middle Eastern

Instructions

Step 1

In a bowl, mix ground meat with finely chopped onions, garlic, parsley, cumin, coriander, paprika, and salt. Add olive oil and knead until well combined.

Step 2

Shape the mixture into small logs or patties and skewer them onto metal or wooden skewers.

Step 3

Preheat a grill to medium-high heat. Grill the kababs for 12-15 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are cooked through and lightly charred on the outside.

Ingredients

1 lb ground lamb (or beef/chicken)

1/2 onion, finely chopped

2 garlic cloves, minced

2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped

1 tsp cumin

1 tsp coriander

1 tsp paprika

Salt and pepper to taste

2 tbsp olive oil
