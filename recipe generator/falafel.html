Falafel

Crispy and delicious fried balls made from ground chickpeas, herbs, and spices.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

5-7 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags: snack vegetarian Middle Eastern

Instructions

Step 1

Mix ground chickpeas, garlic, onions, parsley, cumin, and flour in a bowl. Shape into balls.

Step 2

Heat oil in a pan and fry the falafel balls until golden brown on all sides.

Ingredients

2 cups chickpeas

1 onion

2 cloves garlic

1/2 cup parsley

1 tsp cumin
