Hummus

A classic Middle Eastern dip made from pureed chickpeas, tahini, garlic, and lemon.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

None

serving

4 servings

Tags: dip vegetarian Middle Eastern

Instructions

Step 1

Blend chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and olive oil in a food processor until smooth.

Ingredients

1 can chickpeas

1/4 cup tahini

2 tbsp lemon juice

1 garlic clove

2 tbsp olive oil
