Shawarma

Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern dish made of marinated meat, usually lamb or chicken, roasted on a vertical rotisserie.

prep time

1 hour

cook time

30 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags: meat Middle Eastern chicken

Instructions

Step 1

Marinate chicken with garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, and shawarma spices for 1 hour.

Ingredients

4 chicken breasts

2 tbsp shawarma spice mix

1/4 cup olive oil

2 garlic cloves
