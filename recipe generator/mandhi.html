Mandi

Mandi is a traditional dish from the Arabian Peninsula, made of rice, meat (usually lamb, goat, or chicken), and a blend of spices, cooked in a unique method.

prep time

1 hour

cook time

2 hours

serving

6 servings

Tags: rice lamb Middle Eastern

Instructions

Step 1

Season the meat with a mix of spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, cinnamon, and others). Brown it in a large pot.

Step 2

Add rice, garlic, onions, tomatoes, and more spices, then cook everything together in a sealed pot or oven for 2 hours.

Ingredients

2 lbs lamb or chicken

2 cups basmati rice

1 large onion, chopped

3 cloves garlic, minced

1 tbsp cumin

1 tbsp turmeric

1 tbsp cinnamon
