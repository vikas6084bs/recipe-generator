Camel Biryani

Camel Biryani is a rich, flavorful biryani made with tender pieces of camel meat, aromatic rice, and a complex blend of spices.

prep time

1 hour

cook time

3 hours

serving

6 servings

Tags: meat rice Middle Eastern

Instructions

Step 1

Marinate the camel meat with yogurt, spices, and herbs for at least 1 hour.

Step 2

In a large pot, cook the meat until tender. Then layer it with cooked basmati rice and cook it for 1 hour.

Ingredients

2 lbs camel meat

2 cups basmati rice

1 large onion, chopped

2 tbsp yogurt

1 tbsp garam masala

1 tbsp cumin

1 tsp cardamom
